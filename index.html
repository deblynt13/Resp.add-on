Living in Wellenpark

Many neighborhoods to choose from

Our Welcome Center is a friendly starting place for you to find your next home base. Here, you can learn about Wellen Park’s diverse neighborhoods, community amenities, regional destinations…the list goes on! We can’t wait to say hi.

From boutique shops to live music, all-day eateries to stunning sunsets, there are hundreds of ways to brighten your days. Discover the joys of community living.

Cool Today Park Baseball fans have plenty to cheer about at Wellen Park, he new home of the Spring Training Facility for the Atlanta Braves. When it isn’t hosting games, CoolToday Park is the perfect place for concerts, movie nights, happy hours, and other fun events for the community.

Why Venice?

Are you tired of winter? Winter temps in the Venice area average in the low 70's, and no snow to shovel! You can travel to many areas of florida within a few hours, many can be done as day trips. You can get to the Disney area in about 2 hours or so or make it a weekend getaway to Miami in about 5 hours or so.
