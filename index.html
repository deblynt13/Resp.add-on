Why Venice?

Are you tired of winter? Winter temps in the Venice area average in the low 70's, and no snow to shovel! You can travel to many areas of florida within a few hours, many can be done as day trips. You can get to the Disney area in about 2 hours or so or make it a weekend getaway to Miami in about 5 hours or so.
