Marketplace

Running errands just became a lot easier, with the Marketplace centrally located on Tamiami Trail. Featuring plenty of parking, restaurants, shops, and a Publix grocery store, this plaza has all the perks.

Imagine More Ways to Be Well

From a coal-fired pizza shop to a massive supermarket, the Marketplace is your one-stop-shop for every need and craving. Anchored at U.S. 41 and West Villages Parkway, the Wellen Park Marketplace is a Publix-anchored shopping plaza that includes a contemporary mix of restaurants, shops, and services.
